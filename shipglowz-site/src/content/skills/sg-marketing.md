## Choose a mode

Use `009-sg-marketing` with the mode that matches the unresolved question:

- `market <target>` for demand, competitors, keywords, monetization, or market evidence.
- `gtm <target>` for positioning, offer, funnel, trust, pricing, analytics, or launch readiness.
- `copy <target>` for clarity, tone, readability, CTA, and page-level trust.
- `copywriting <target>` for persona, offer, objections, persuasion, and conversion structure.

If the real need is generic cited research, start with `203-sg-research`. For raw URLs or incoming signals, start with `205-sg-veille`. SEO stays with `406-sg-seo`; email sequences stay with `emailing`; broader content planning, drafting, or publishing stays with `007-sg-content`.
