# 201-sg-enrich

> Upgrade existing content with fresh research, better structure, and stronger conversion intent without turning it into generic AI filler.

## What It Does

`201-sg-enrich` takes an existing article, page, or content folder and makes it more useful, more current, and more actionable. It researches the topic, checks technical accuracy, fills content gaps, and rewrites the piece so a reader can scan it, trust it, and know what to do next.

This is especially valuable for solo founders publishing content that needs to support SEO, AI visibility, credibility, and conversion at the same time.

## Who It's For

- Solo founders running content-led growth
- Product builders maintaining docs, landing pages, or editorial content
- Teams that already have content, but know it is stale, thin, or too generic

## When To Use It

- when a blog post needs fresher data or better examples
- when a landing page is accurate but not persuasive enough
- when a content folder needs a quality pass before promotion
- when you want stronger structure for AI overviews and search extraction

## What You Give It

- a file path to a page or article
- or a folder containing content files
- ideally existing business and brand context for better targeting

## What You Get Back

- updated content in the original language
- fresher references, clearer structure, and stronger calls to action
- explicit warnings when business or brand context is missing
- a report of what changed and where confidence is limited

## Typical Examples

```bash
/201-sg-enrich src/content/blog/pricing-page-mistakes.md
/201-sg-enrich src/content/blog
```

## Limits

`201-sg-enrich` improves existing content; it is not the best starting point for creating net-new long-form content from scratch. It also depends on the quality of the current article, business context, and available primary sources.

## Related Skills

- `200-sg-redact` for original long-form content creation
- `300-sg-docs` for product and technical documentation
- `204-sg-market-study` when the topic strategy itself is still unclear
